Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious lasagna recipe made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, brown the ground beef and drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to the pan and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles and a layer of cheese mixture. Repeat the layers.
  6. Bake in the oven at 190Â°C for 30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
