Chicken Breast

Description

This is a tasty recipe made with fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Garlic powder
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Red bell pepper
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pasta
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breast with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the chicken until golden brown.
  3. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, saut√© onion and red bell pepper until softened.
  5. Add tomato sauce and let it simmer for a few minutes.
  6. Cook pasta according to the package instructions.
  7. Slice the cooked chicken and add it to the tomato sauce mixture.
  8. Serve the chicken and sauce over the cooked pasta.
  9. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and enjoy!
