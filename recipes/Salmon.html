Salmon smoked

Description

This is another delicious recipe with a unique flavor.

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • Lemon
  • Dill
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Asparagus
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Balsamic glaze

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt, pepper, and dill.
  3. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the salmon.
  4. Melt butter with minced garlic in a skillet.
  5. Sear the salmon in the skillet for a few minutes on each side.
  6. Transfer the salmon to a baking dish and bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes.
  7. In the same skillet, sauté asparagus and cherry tomatoes until tender.
  8. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the cooked vegetables.
  9. Serve the salmon with the sautéed vegetables.
  10. Garnish with fresh dill and enjoy!
